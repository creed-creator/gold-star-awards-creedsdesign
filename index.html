Our story

More than a trophy shop — we love what we do.

Gold Star Awards & Engraving has been part of the Wasatch Front community since 1985. Our showroom in Layton is stocked with an extraordinary variety of awards, gifts, and keepsakes — and we take real pride in every piece that leaves our shop.

On any given week we might be crafting trophies for a youth soccer league, engraving a retirement plaque for a 30-year employee, laser-engraving a set of custom rocks for the Utah Highway Patrol, or personalizing waffle irons for a creative ad agency. The variety keeps us energized — and the results keep our customers coming back.

We have customers who drive past other shops all the way from Salt Lake City because they trust us to get it right. Rush service is always available, and we treat every order — large or small — with the same care.
